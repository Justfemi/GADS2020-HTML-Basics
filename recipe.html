Hot Cocoa Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 Table spoon of Wired Brain Coffee Hot Cocoa Mix
  • 1 litre of milk
  • Marshmallows (optional)
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Scoop Hot Cocoa Mix into a mug.
  2. Heat milk on a stove or in a microwave for a about 10 minutes.
  3. Pour milk into the mug and stir gently.
  4. Stir until mix is fully dissolves in the milk.
  5. Add marshmallow if you have.
  6. Top with seasoning to taste.

Cocoa Facts

Do you know the difference?
Cocoa
Cocoa is the powdered substance left after processing cacao beans and has little or now cacao butter.
Chocolate
Chocolate is basically cocoa with the cacao butter and often sugar and milk.
